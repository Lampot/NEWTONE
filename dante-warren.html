dante warren

dante warren is an artist moving inside the newtone world. the work lives between feeling and fracture, melody and silence, saying enough without explaining too much.

his music is not designed to arrive clean. it drifts, it lingers, it leaves gaps on purpose. some songs feel unfinished. some feel too honest. that tension is the point.

with newtone, dante exists as part of a wider signal. not a product, not a persona. just motion, sound, and intention moving forward without asking permission. Currently Dante Warren has 1 song out called THIS IS MAGIC with DYLN. Listen to dante’s latest track "THIS IS MAGIC" on Spotify

newtone is a space for artists who don’t resolve. who sit inside uncertainty. who let the work speak quietly. Contact me at livelampot@gmail.com
